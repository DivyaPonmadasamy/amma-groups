Waterproofing Solutions

Diaphragm Wall (D-Wall) & Retaining Wall Leak Control

Basement diaphragm walls and retaining walls are critical structural elements designed to resist earth pressure and groundwater ingress. However, over time, these systems can develop leakage through construction joints, tie rod holes, honeycombing, shrinkage cracks, and other construction-related defects.

If left untreated, such leakages can progressively worsen, leading to dampness, corrosion of reinforcement, reduction in structural durability, and functional issues within the basement space.

Sealing Strength Below Ground

Advanced Waterproofing and Grouting Solutions for Underground and Below-Grade Structures with Targeted Leakage Control and Long-Term Performance

At our organization, we specialize in advanced waterproofing and grouting solutions tailored specifically for underground and below-grade structures. Our experienced technical team conducts detailed site inspections to identify the root cause of leakage and selects the most effective repair methodology to ensure long-lasting and reliable performance.

Our range of specialized services includes diaphragm wall joint sealing, where leakage at panel joints is treated using high-performance injection systems combined with water-reactive grouts.

For retaining wall leakage treatment, we apply systematic sealing techniques that effectively prevent water ingress while preserving the structural integrity of the wall.

Crack injection grouting is a key part of our expertise, involving the injection of specialized grout materials into cracks to restore structural continuity and eliminate seepage paths. This method is highly effective for both active and dormant cracks and provides a permanent solution when executed with the right materials and techniques.

We also undertake honeycomb repair and sealing, addressing voids and porous zones in concrete caused by improper compaction or workmanship issues. These defects are treated using advanced repair mortars and injection systems to restore the density, strength, and impermeability of the concrete.

Water stopping at construction joints is another critical service, where we prevent leakage at the interface of concrete pours through the use of injection grouts, sealing systems, and water barrier technologies designed to block water pathways effectively.

In addition, we provide comprehensive basement slab and wall seepage control solutions, including pressure grouting and integrated waterproofing systems capable of resisting hydrostatic pressure and preventing recurring leakage.

A key advantage of our service lies in the use of our own in-house manufactured admixtures and grouting materials. We have developed specialized formulations that are specifically designed to control and arrest water ingress at any stage—whether it is active leakage, damp seepage, or high-pressuregroundwater flow.

We maintain complete control over the quality, consistency, and performance of the materials as they are produced by our own company. This enables us to customize solutions based on actual site conditions, water pressure levels, and the severity of leakage. Our ability to adapt our materials and methods ensures that even the most challenging and complex leakage problems are handled with confidence and precision.

By combining advanced in-house materials, technical expertise, and proven application methods, we deliver long-lasting waterproofing solutions that enhance structural durability, protect against water damage, and extend the service life of your infrastructure. Whether for new construction or rehabilitation of existing structures, our solutions are designed to perform reliably under all site conditions.
