Engineering Excellence Since 1974

Who we are

AmmA Groups is a well-established Indian engineering and trading organization with a legacy of excellence dating back to 1974. Founded by the visionary Mr. Arumugam Kuppan Mastry, the group was built on a strong foundation of technical expertise, integrity, and commitment to quality construction practices, growing into a multi-vertical engineering and trading group serving complex construction needs.

What sets us apart

Over the past five decades, AmmA Groups has evolved into a multi-disciplinary engineering group, delivering specialized solutions in civil engineering, structural rehabilitation, basement waterproofing, deep structure grouting, and underground construction technologies.

Our extensive industry experience and technical capability allow us to handle complex structural challenges, particularly in basements, diaphragm walls, retaining structures, and marine environments.

Leadership Through Third Generations

The organization represents a proud third-generation engineering legacy.

Mr. Arumugam Kuppan Mastry

First Generation (1974)

Founded by Arumugam, a visionary builder with strong field expertise, he established the foundation of AmmA Groups through disciplined engineering practices and a reputation for dependable workmanship. During this formative period, he executed several significant commercial buildings across South India and successfully delivered projects for public-sector and institutional clients. His portfolio included construction works associated with government infrastructure such as bus terminals and facilities linked to public administration. Through hands-on leadership, technical precision, and trust-based relationships, he laid the structural and ethical framework that continues to guide the organization today.

Mr. Nanda Kumaar Arumugam

Second Generation

Nanda transformed the organization into a diversified multi-group enterprise through innovative ideas and advanced technologies, formally shaping the modern AmmA Groups.

Third Generation Leadership

Mr. Monish Adhith leads nationwide operations across India, while Mr. Sanjith Sarvesh manages procurement and Maldives projects. With its registered office in Tamil Nadu, India, AmmA Groups continues to uphold its founding values of quality, durability, and customer satisfaction.

Development into the Maldives

In 2023, the Group expanded its global footprint by establishing operations in the Maldives through AmmA Tradder. In Maldives Male, AmmA Groups is supported by one of the famous leading local construction companies RCC- Rasheed Carpentry and Construction Pvt Ltd. Through this collaboration, the Group has undertaken specialized structural rehabilitation works for island resort infrastructure, including,

  • Overwater villas / water villas
  • Walkway decks
  • Arrival jetties
  • Arrival pavilions
  • Service jetties

Our solutions are backed by a lifetime guarantee for structural rehabilitation works.

N59 CORROS GUARD

Reinforcement Anti-Corrosion Protection.

As part of our continuous innovation for aggressive marine environments, AmmA Groups is introducing N59 CORROS GUARD, our proprietary reinforcement anti-corrosion protection system specially engineered for Maldives conditions.

Designed to withstand high salinity, humidity, and coastal exposure, N59 CORROS GUARD enhances structural durability, extends service life, and reduces long-term maintenance requirements. The system is further backed by a research certification from the Indian Corrosion.
