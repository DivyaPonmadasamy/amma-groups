Innovation in Construction Materials

N59 CORROS GUARD

What is N59

N59 CORROS GUARD is a proprietary reinforcement protection system developed by AmmA Groups to safeguard concrete structures in harsh marine environments such as the Maldives, Indonesia.

Engineered to resist high salinity, humidity, and coastal exposure, it protects reinforcing steel from corrosion, significantly improving structural durability.

Backed by research certification from the Indian Corrosion Department, N59 CORROS GUARD enhances the service life of structures while minimizing long-term maintenance costs.
